ANJALAI AMMAL MAHALINGAM ENGINEERING COLLEGE, KOVILVENNI

Anjalai Ammal Mahalingam Engineering College (AAMEC) is a college in Kovilvenni near Needamangalam, Thiruvarur, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1995, it offers higher education in engineering and management. Anjalai Ammal Mahalingam Engineering College was affiliated with Anna University Trichy. The college was approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). AAMEC is on NH 67. It has a railway station at the back entrance of college.
